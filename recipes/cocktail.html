How to make the gin and jam cocktail


This gin and jam cocktail, refreshing and fruity, is a versatile gin cocktail that is sure to become your new summer favorite. Use any flavor jam you like!


Ingredients


  • 2 fluid ounces gin
  • 1 tablespoon jam,blackberry or any flavor
  • 3/4 fluid ounces fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 fluid ounces simple syrup
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • 1 mint leaf,for garnish
  • 1 splash club soda

  1. Place gin, jam, lemon juice,and simple syrup in a cocktail shaker, along with 1 cup ice cubes. Seal and shake vigorously until outside is frosted, 10 to 15 seconds.
  2. Fill a glass with crushed ice; strain cocktail into the glass. Top with a splash of club soda, if desired. Garnish with mint leaf.
